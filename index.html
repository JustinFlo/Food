Filipino Sinigang

This up here is Sinigang, A Famous Filipino Soup

This soup is famous for being great tangy yet flavorful soup. Along with it being flavorful, there are multiple reasons why this is considered to be the most famous filipino soup. It is one of my all-time favorites

The broth is a nice savory and sour taste that will have your tastebuds vibrate.

This has always been a childhood dish of mine, my mother would create this masterpiece and always have me to taste it to my likings. Out of all my family, I had loved this to be my #1 dish

  • It is known to be a special comfort food in the Philippines
  • Due to it being so hot yet tangy, it helps warms the body by a increased amount
  • Multiple varations of the dish, can be made with anything
  • Anything can be added to the dish due to it's great tangy flavor.
