This is Korean Hotpot

This has to be a delicacy due to the absolute creativity this dish has. It isn't exactly a dish but multiple dishes in one.

Pros:

  • Any form of food in the major catagories can be used. From meat, to seafood, to even vegtables, anything can be used.
  • The food is not limited to Korean food. It can be used for any food.
  • The broth itself can be used as a soup for rice or for basing any large dish.
  • Anyone can be able to cook the food. It is easy for anyone to cook the food as you don't need to fully cook any of it.
  • It can be cooked for only one person or for a group of people.
